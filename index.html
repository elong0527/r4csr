Welcome!

Welcome to R for clinical study report and submission. Clinical study reports (CSR) are essential parts of clinical trials development. a CSR is an “integrated” full scientific report of an individual clinical trial.

The ICH E3: structure and content of clinical study reports provide guidance to assist sponsors in the development of a CSR. In this book, you will learn how to use R to prepare CSR and submit to regulatory agencies.

This is the work-in-progress draft of the book.
